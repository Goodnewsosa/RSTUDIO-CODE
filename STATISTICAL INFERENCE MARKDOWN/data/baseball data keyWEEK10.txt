Baseball data key

Name: Player name.
AtBat: Number of at-bats for the player.
Hits: Number of hits the player achieved.
HmRun: Number of home runs hit by the player.
Runs: Number of runs scored by the player.
RBI: Runs batted in by the player.
Walks: Number of walks received by the player.
Years: Number of years played in the league.
CAtBat: Career at-bats for the player.
CHits: Career hits for the player.
CHmRun: Career home runs for the player.
CRuns: Career runs scored by the player.
CRBI: Career runs batted in by the player.
CWalks: Career walks received by the player.
League: League the player is in ('A' = American League, 'N' = National League).
Division: Division in which the player's team plays.
PutOuts: Number of putouts by the player.
Assists: Number of assists by the player.
Errors: Number of errors committed by the player.
Salary: Salary of the player.
NewLeague: League the player is assigned to after a salary raise or change ('A' = American League, 'N' = National League).
